What is DIDAS All About? 🤔

DIDAS is a modern, intuitive time tracking system designed specifically for educators, facilitators, and staff members who need a reliable way to record their work hours.

Think of it as your digital time card or virtual punch clock that simplifies attendance tracking with just a few clicks.

How It Works 🕒

📝 Simple Registration

Create your account in seconds with basic information - name, email, ID number, and a secure password.

🔐 Secure Login

Access your personal dashboard with your email and password. Your data is protected with secure encryption.

⏰ One-Click Time Tracking

Click "Time In" when you start working and "Time Out" when you finish. The system handles everything else automatically.

📊 Automatic Record Keeping

All your work sessions are automatically saved with precise timestamps and calculated durations.

📈 Comprehensive History

View your complete attendance history with detailed records of when you started, finished, and how long you worked.

📱 User-Friendly Dashboard

An intuitive interface shows your current status, elapsed time, and quick statistics about your attendance.
